Missing Link in the Method

Irreverent Acting
The gap in the Stanislavsky/Strasberg system caused by the failure to address instrumental preparation — the need to free the actor's emotional instrument before craft techniques can work. Morris identifies BEING as the missing link that bridges theory and application.

Actors who were naturally emotionally free succeeded with the Method; those with emotional blocks could not apply it. Stanislavsky himself admitted his approach was incomplete. Without instrumental preparation, even valid craft techniques are useless for actors with blocks. Morris's first two books addressed this gap directly.
