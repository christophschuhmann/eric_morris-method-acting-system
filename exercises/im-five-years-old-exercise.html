'I'm Five Years Old' Exercise Instrumental

Ultimate Consciousness
Goal

Access unconscious emotional storage from childhood to increase vulnerability, affectability, and depth of emotional availability.

How To

State 'I'm five years old and I...' and stream consciousness from that age — places, people, experiences, feelings. Use first person present tense as much as possible to make it immediate. After exploring that age, advance year by year or jump to key ages. Do not censor or analyze what comes up.

Who Should Do It

All actors; particularly those who have difficulty accessing vulnerability or childhood emotional states. Excellent for actors who feel disconnected from their own history.

Why It Works

Childhood experiences are stored in the unconscious with great emotional intensity. The exercise systematically unlocks these stored emotions, increasing the actor's vulnerability and range. Practice enriches dreams as a side effect.

Additional Info

With practice, the content becomes deeper and reveals more vulnerability and emotional facets. Making it part of a daily workout builds a comfortable pathway to the unconscious.
