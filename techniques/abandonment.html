Abandonment Both

Irreverent Acting
Lying on the floor and having an all-out tantrum—moving the body with abandonment in all directions with no physical interference or leadership, while screaming and making huge uninterrupted vocal expurgations.
Instrumental Use

Purges tension and conglomerated impulses. Done until tension is purged.

Craft Use

Used with specific content to build a foundation for volatile, irrational character behavior. Must have a specific goal: what emotional state to stimulate and what content the Abandonment must have.

When to Use

When the character is highly volatile and irrational; as an instrumental tension-purger. Not appropriate for subtle emotional states.
