Perspective Inventory (Technique) Instrumental

Imaging & Unconscious
A progressive multi-week exercise that systematically walks backward through the actor's life, week by week, month by month, year by year, to recover early childhood memories and unlock unconscious material.
Instrumental Use

Works as a primer/lever to pry open sealed memory boxes. Each meaningful remembered event brings surrounding memories with it. Progressively opens access to experiences from age 3 and younger—material that may include prenatal responses. Used as a foundation for 'I'm five years old and I…' work. Actors have reported remembering their birth through this process.

Craft Use

Not primarily a craft technique; primarily instrumental/developmental.

When to Use

As a regular weekly practice over months. Also: when doing the Abstract Places from Childhood area of Unconscious Suggestions.
